# Age-inclusive UX

- Large touch targets (`--madia-touch-min: 44px`)
- Municipality list alternative to map interaction
- First mobile map tap selects; explicit Explore navigates
- Simplified and high-contrast modes via `useAccessibilityPreferences`
- Help and skippable onboarding architecture (`/help`)
- Plain-language verification and price labels

See master specification section 6 and `docs/UX_RESEARCH_PLAN.md`.
